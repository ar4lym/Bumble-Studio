Our Team

A dedicated group of creators combining technical skill, artistry, and vision to bring Pattern of Thought to life.

Realising the gap in the understanding of OCD and what it really is, often passed off as something minor to non-OCD people, results in those who suffer from OCD being misunderstood, overlooked, and at times dismissed. This lack of awareness can make their struggles feel invisible, even though the condition deeply impacts their daily lives.

Our Game

Experience life through the eyes of someone living with OCD, and see how it can affect their daily routines and interactions.

Our Mission

To create empathy and understanding by immersing players in the challenges faced by individuals with OCD.

Our Goal

We hope players will learn more about OCD and help reduce stigma by fostering compassion and awareness.
